Lynica Publishing Goals

Lynica Publishing strives to provide a place for up-and-coming writers to get their work published at low expense. Guest published authors will publish works to encourage inexperienced writers to get their work out there! I will also use Lynica Publishing as a personal portfolio publishing method. You can read more of my work on the content page, along with other writer's works to inspire you to just do something with your talent and skills.
