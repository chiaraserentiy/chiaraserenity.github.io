Recently Featured Poem by Benjamin Loden

I want to make it sound like me,
But I want to have nice imagery.
I'm not very good at poetry.
Maybe this just isn't for me.

But if, for even just one day,
I finally found what I want to say,
And it stayed around, long enough to know em'
I think I'd call it a poem.
